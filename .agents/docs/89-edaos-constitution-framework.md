## 2. The 5 Constitutional Articles

- **Article 1 (No Hidden Execution)**: Every mutation or capability execution must be logged in the Execution Journal. Unlogged side-effects are strictly forbidden.
- **Article 2 (No Untraceable Decision)**: Every decision proposal must be backed by a verified Level-2 Evidence DAG node. Unproven claims are automatically rejected.
- **Article 3 (No Unsigned Mutation)**: Every action must carry a valid cryptographic signature from a verified Identity Chain (`73-identity-federation-layer.yml`).
- **Article 4 (Human Ultimate Veto)**: Human Engineers retain absolute veto and instant rollback power over all autonomous agents.
- **Article 5 (Zero Data Loss Invariant)**: Saga Rollback safety must be mathematically proven before executing any state-changing mutation.
